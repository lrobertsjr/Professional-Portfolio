Paris, France

Summer 2019 7 Days

A week in the City of Light provided the perfect blend of iconic landmarks and hidden gems. From sunrise at the Eiffel Tower to evening strolls along the Seine, Paris delivered unforgettable moments at every turn.

Highlights

  • Exploring the Louvre's vast collections
  • Morning pastries at a local boulangerie
  • Discovering Montmartre's artistic legacy
  • Picnic in the Luxembourg Gardens

Rome, Italy

Spring 2018 5 Days

Walking through Rome is like stepping into a living museum. The eternal city captivated me with its ancient architecture, vibrant street life, and of course, the incomparable Italian cuisine.

Highlights

  • Standing in the ancient Colosseum
  • Making a wish at the Trevi Fountain
  • Exploring Vatican City and the Sistine Chapel
  • Sampling gelato at a different shop each day

Tokyo, Japan

Fall 2019 10 Days

Tokyo is a city of captivating contrasts – ancient traditions alongside futuristic innovation. From the serene temples to the bustling Shibuya crossing, every experience offered a glimpse into Japan's unique culture.

Highlights

  • Walking through the peaceful Meiji Shrine
  • Experiencing the sensory overload of Akihabara
  • Watching the sunrise from Tokyo Skytree
  • Taking a sushi-making class with a local chef

"Tokyo may have more money and Kyoto more culture; Nara may have more history and Kobe more fashion; but Osaka has the biggest heart."

— Vikas Swarup
