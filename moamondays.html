GLP-1

Anti-diabetic & Weight Management

Integrase Inhibitors

Integrase Inhibitors

HIV Prevention & Treatment

Integrase inhibitors like Bictegravir, Raltegravir and Cabotegravir block the HIV integrase enzyme, preventing the integration of viral DNA into the host genome.

Key Targets:
  • HIV Integrase Enzyme
  • Viral DNA Integration

MMR Vaccine

Live Attenuated Viral Vaccine

The MMR vaccine contains live attenuated strains of measles, mumps, and rubella viruses. It stimulates the immune system to produce antibodies against these viruses, providing long-term immunity.

Narcan

Opioid Antagonist

Narcan

Opioid Antagonist

Narcan (Naloxone) is an opioid antagonist that reverses opioid overdose by competitively binding to opioid receptors in the brain, blocking the effects of opioids like heroin and fentanyl.

Key Targets:
  • Opioid Receptors (Mu, Kappa, Delta)
  • Central Nervous System
  • Respiratory Depression

Alzheimer's Diagnostic Test

Lumipulse G Blood Test

Alzheimer's Diagnostic Test

The Lumipulse G blood test detects amyloid-beta and tau proteins associated with Alzheimer's disease. It uses chemiluminescent technology to measure these biomarkers, aiding in early diagnosis.

Key Targets:
  • Amyloid-beta Protein
  • Tau Protein

Blujepa (Gepotidacin)

Triazaacenaphthylene Antibiotic

Blujepa (Gepotidacin)

Triazaacenaphthylene Antibiotic

Blujepa (Gepotidacin) is a fully synthetic triazaacenaphthylene antibiotic that inhibits bacterial DNA replication by targeting the DNA gyrase enzyme. It is effective against a broad range of Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria.

Key Targets:
  • DNA Gyrase & Topoisomerase IV
  • Bacterial DNA Replication
  • Gram-positive and Gram-negative Bacteria

GMRx2 (Widaplik)

Antihypertensive

GMRx2 (Widaplik) is a novel antihypertensive agent that works by combining 3 mechanisms: inhibiting the renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system (RAAS), promoting vasodilation, and enhancing diuresis.

Lenacapavir (Yeztugo)

HIV Prevention

Lenacapavir (Yeztugo) is a first-in-class HIV capsid inhibitor that blocks the HIV capsid protein, preventing viral replication and spread. It is used for both treatment and prevention of HIV.

Anzupgo (Delgocitinib)

Topical JAK Inhibitor

Anzupgo (Delgocitinib)

Topical JAK Inhibitor

Anzupgo (Delgocitinib) is a topical Janus kinase (JAK) inhibitor used to treat inflammatory skin conditions like atopic dermatitis. It works by blocking the JAK-STAT signaling pathway, reducing inflammation and itching.

Key Targets:
  • JAK Enzymes (JAK1, JAK2, JAK3)
  • STAT Proteins
  • Inflammatory Cytokines
