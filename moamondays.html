Metformin

Oral Anti-diabetic

Metformin

Biguanide Anti-diabetic

Metformin reduces hepatic glucose production by inhibiting the mitochondrial respiratory chain, activating AMPK. This leads to decreased gluconeogenesis, increased glucose uptake in muscle, and improved insulin sensitivity.

Key Targets:
  • Mitochondrial Complex I
  • AMPK Pathway
  • Hepatic Glucose Production

Atorvastatin

Statin

Atorvastatin

HMG-CoA Reductase Inhibitor

Atorvastatin competitively inhibits HMG-CoA reductase, the rate-limiting enzyme in cholesterol synthesis. This decreases hepatic cholesterol production, upregulates LDL receptors, and enhances clearance of LDL from circulation.

Key Targets:
  • HMG-CoA Reductase
  • Mevalonate Pathway
  • LDL Receptor Expression

Semaglutide

GLP-1 Receptor Agonist

Semaglutide

GLP-1 Receptor Agonist

Semaglutide mimics the incretin GLP-1, binding to and activating the GLP-1 receptor. This stimulates glucose-dependent insulin secretion, suppresses glucagon, slows gastric emptying, and promotes satiety through central effects.

Key Targets:
  • GLP-1 Receptor
  • Pancreatic Î²-cells
  • Hypothalamic Appetite Centers

Lisinopril

ACE Inhibitor

Lisinopril

Angiotensin-Converting Enzyme Inhibitor

Lisinopril inhibits ACE, preventing the conversion of angiotensin I to angiotensin II. This reduces vasoconstriction and aldosterone secretion, leading to vasodilation, decreased blood pressure, and reduced sodium retention.

Key Targets:
  • Angiotensin-Converting Enzyme
  • Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System
  • Bradykinin Metabolism

Fluoxetine

SSRI

Fluoxetine

Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor

Fluoxetine selectively inhibits the serotonin transporter (SERT), blocking serotonin reuptake into presynaptic neurons. This increases serotonin concentration in the synaptic cleft, enhancing serotonergic neurotransmission.

Key Targets:
  • Serotonin Transporter (SERT)
  • Synaptic Serotonin Levels
  • Serotonergic Neurotransmission

Adalimumab

TNF Inhibitor

Adalimumab

TNF-Î± Monoclonal Antibody

Adalimumab is a fully human IgG1 monoclonal antibody that specifically binds to TNF-Î±, preventing it from interacting with cell surface TNF receptors. This inhibits pro-inflammatory signaling cascades in autoimmune conditions.

Key Targets:
  • Tumor Necrosis Factor-Î±
  • Inflammatory Cytokine Cascade
  • Immune Cell Activation
