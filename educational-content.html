Presentation Skills

Create and deliver presentations that engage, inform, and inspire your audience

The S.T.A.R. Presentation Framework

S - Story

Begin with a compelling narrative that captures attention and establishes relevance

  • Use personal anecdotes or case studies
  • Create emotional connection
  • Establish the "why" behind your message

T - Teach

Present your core content in a clear, structured, and digestible format

  • Use the rule of three for main points
  • Support with relevant data and visuals
  • Break complex ideas into simpler components

A - Apply

Demonstrate practical applications and relevance to your audience

  • Include real-world examples
  • Address specific audience challenges
  • Provide actionable takeaways

R - Reinforce

Strengthen key messages and ensure they stick with your audience

  • Summarize main points
  • Circle back to opening story
  • End with a clear call to action

Design Tips

  • Use high-quality, relevant images
  • Limit text to 6 lines per slide
  • Maintain consistent visual style
  • Choose readable fonts (min. 24pt)
  • Use contrasting colors for text/background
  • Include only one key idea per slide

Delivery Techniques

  • Practice with a timer
  • Record yourself to identify areas for improvement
  • Use deliberate pauses for emphasis
  • Maintain eye contact with different audience members
  • Incorporate appropriate gestures
  • Modulate your voice to maintain engagement
